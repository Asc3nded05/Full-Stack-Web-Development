Growing up I've always been fascinated by computers and the wide array of diverse experiences
they can be used to create. In my sophomore year at West Irondequoit High School, I took my
first computer science class where I finally got to take a peek behind the curtain and start
my journey to understand how to use computers to create experiences of my own. Since then I

Houghton University. Through this process I've used Java, Python, SQL and more to learn how to
create applications, simple servers, and databases. After finishing my Bachelors Degree at
Houghton University, I am planning on pursuing a Masters Degree in Computer Science with the
hopes of entering the software engineering market after schooling. In the meantime, I am
currently researching summer internship opportunities to grow my experience and skills.
